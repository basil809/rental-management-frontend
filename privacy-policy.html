Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to La Maison, a web-based rental management system designed to help tenants, landlords, and property managers manage rental properties efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username, email, phone number, and password.
  • Property Data: Details of properties you list, manage, or rent.
  • Usage Data: Pages you visit, time spent on the site, and actions you take.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your browser that help us recognize you and improve your experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Manage your account and provide platform services.
  • Improve system performance and user experience.
  • Communicate important updates and security notifications.
  • Comply with legal requirements and prevent fraud.

4. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your login session so you stay signed in.
  • Save your preferences such as language or theme.
  • Collect anonymous analytics to improve performance.

You can choose to accept or reject cookies using our Cookie Consent Banner or by changing your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website features.

5. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All user data is transmitted securely using HTTPS encryption and stored on protected servers. We also use authentication tokens and encrypted cookies to maintain secure sessions.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (e.g., Google Analytics) for usage statistics. These tools may set cookies to collect anonymous data. You can opt out of analytics by rejecting non-essential cookies in our cookie consent banner.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw cookie consent at any time by clearing browser cookies.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
